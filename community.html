MERCURY



DYNAMIC COMMUNITY SPACE FOR CREATIVE WOMEN

Mercury is a contemporary, feminine brand with the following brand vision: “In a society of constant judgement and self-scrutiny, we believe we should celebrate our creative self-expression and take pride in our identity rather than condemn or criticize, because only then can we exist freely and truly love ourselves.” Mercury values self-expression, freedom, self-love and being bold. Mercury wants to offer creative individuals a free platform to express themselves and seek inspiration.

We want to create a safe space for women to come together and empower each other to be better and love themselves.
